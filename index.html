Welcome to Rivals Tactics

  • 🎤 Voice input — This app runs by voice. Click Power Up Voice Link and just talk (works in Edge/Chrome): call out enemy heroes as you see them, say “we have <hero>” for your own team, and “<hero> ultimate” to track a big ult. Hit Full Voice Command Playbook for everything you can say.
  • Counter Intel — As you build the enemy team, the moment it's full (6 heroes) counter-pick suggestions appear automatically under Enemy Squad.
  • Ult Watch — Tracks enemy ultimate charge with countdown rings so you know when a big ult is about to drop.
  • No mic? Backup plan — If you don't have a mic or hit any trouble with the voice commands, the Roster on the right is your backup: click a hero (using the My / Enemy toggle) to assign them.

🎤 Voice Command Playbook

Click Power Up Voice Link, then just talk — no need to pause between commands. Works in Edge or Chrome (not the in-app preview). Hero nicknames work too (“Cap”, “Spidey”, “Tony”, “Luna”…).

Build your squads

Just say a hero’s name — “Venom”Adds to the enemy squad (the default while you call out the match)
“We have Groot” / “our Groot” / “my team Groot”Adds to your squad
“They have Hela” / “their Hela”Adds to the enemy squad

Deadpool (flex pick)

“Deadpool tank”Adds Deadpool as a Vanguard
“Deadpool duelist” / “Deadpool DPS”Adds Deadpool as a Duelist
“Healpool” / “Deadpool healer” / “Deadpool strat”Adds Deadpool as a Strategist

Remove & swap

“Remove Venom”Removes from the enemy squad
“Remove Groot from my team”Removes from your squad
“Remove Hela from their”Removes from the enemy squad
“They swapped Venom for Magneto”Swaps the enemy hero out for the new one

Clear a squad

“Clear”Clears the enemy squad (default)
“Clear my team” / “clear my squad”Clears your squad
“Clear enemy team”Clears the enemy squad

Ult timers

“Hela ultimate”Starts that hero’s ult countdown ring in Ult Watch
“Punisher ultimate” / “puni ultimate” / “pun ultimate”Starts Punisher’s ult timer
“Hela timer” / “Hela charged”Backup trigger words — longer words the speech engine almost never misses
“Ult” slang works too“Ult / ulted / ulting” count, even when they mis-hear as “alt / alted / alting” — “ultimate” is still the most reliable

Finalize

“Lock it in”Reads the counter-pick intel out loud (it also appears on screen the moment the enemy squad hits 6)

Tips

“speak back” checkboxTurn on for spoken confirmations so you never look away from the game
Mishears happenGlance at the roster for the exact name the app expects, or check the “heard” readout under the button
